Coaching

As an executive coach and digital innovation strategist, I partner with leaders of purpose-driven organisations to build high-performance cultures, embed innovation, and deliver measurable impact for people, nature and business. With over 20 years’ experience across environmental consulting, public sector bodies, NGOs and impact-focused enterprises, I bring a rare blend of strategic leadership, scientific rigour and practical transformation expertise.

If you would like to explore coaching with me, please get in contact.
