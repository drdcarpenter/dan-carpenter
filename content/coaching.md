As an executive coach and digital innovation strategist, I partner with leaders of purpose-driven organisations to build high-performance cultures, embed innovation, and deliver measurable impact for people, nature and business. With over 20 years’ experience across environmental consulting, public sector bodies, NGOs and impact-focused enterprises, I bring a rare blend of strategic leadership, scientific rigour and practical transformation expertise.

If you would like to explore coaching with me, please get in contact.

# Areas of Coaching Delivery

## Innovation & Digital Transformation Leadership
Supporting leaders to confidently navigate digital change and embed innovation in complex organisations
* Embedding digital strategy within purpose-led businesses
* Leading technology adoption (GIS, automation, data systems, AI-enabled tools)
* Building innovation cultures that move from ideas to execution
* Aligning digital transformation with organisational mission and impact
* Reducing resistance to change and strengthening team buy-in

## Purpose-Driven & Impact Leadership
Coaching leaders in environmental, sustainability and mission-led sectors to balance performance with purpose
* Clarifying organisational purpose and strategic direction
* Leading with values while maintaining commercial discipline
* Scaling impact without compromising integrity
* Navigating stakeholder complexity (boards, regulators, communities, investors)
* Building leadership confidence in mission-critical roles

## Strategic Thinking & Decision-Making
Helping executives develop clarity, systems thinking and evidence-based leadership
* Strategic planning in uncertain or regulated environments
* Data-informed decision-making
* Systems thinking for complex ecological and organisational challenges
* Managing risk in growth and transformation
* Board-level communication and influence

## Culture & High-Performance Teams
Enabling leaders to build resilient, motivated and accountable teams.
* Creating psychologically safe, innovation-ready cultures
* Leading technical and specialist teams effectively
* Strengthening accountability and ownership
* Managing high-performing experts and knowledge workers
* Developing future leaders within purpose-driven organisations

## Founder & Technical Leader Transition Coaching
Supporting scientists, technical specialists and founders stepping into leadership roles.
* Transition from subject-matter expert to strategic leader
* Developing executive presence
* Delegation and scaling leadership capacity
* Avoiding burnout in high-responsibility roles
* Building sustainable leadership habits

## Biodiversity, Natural Capital & Environmental Sector Leadership
Sector-specialist coaching for leaders operating in environmental and sustainability markets.
* Navigating Biodiversity Net Gain and natural capital frameworks
* Aligning ecological integrity with commercial strategy
* Leading in regulated and policy-driven environments
* Innovation in environmental consultancy and monitoring
* Building digitally enabled environmental enterprises
